About us

CEO

Victor Mosquera

"A well-prepared meal has delicate flavors that must be retained in the mouth to be appreciated." Victor Mosquera (2015).

It all started with an idea, a purpose to teach people how to prepare dishes with the food that people usually throw away because it doesn't look as good as the day they bought it. At the beginning it was a distant goal since it was not a topic that was very much covered in social networks, but the positive side was that it would give our app the opportunity to shine and show all that we wanted to address in the application.
