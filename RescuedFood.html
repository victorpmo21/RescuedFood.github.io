Complete Guides

All Rescued Food guides use cutting-edge AI technology to get all kinds of information online about food waste or rescued food. Whenever you enter the guides, you will see many features, such as:

The guides have example images for better reading and better visualization.

The guides have a setting in which you can adapt to the color you like best.

The guides have features such as that they can be downloaded for reading in case you do not have internet connection and also the option to be able to share them.

Efficient videos

Our videos run smoothly in the background, providing maximum viewing while using minimal resources on your device. Rescued Food videos consistently deliver the best information on food waste and rescued food. In addition, they offer:

Rescued Food videos are an effective way to visually report information on various topics. They can show demonstrations, tutorials, graphics, maps, and more, making it easy to understand complex concepts.

Rescued Food videos can also be educational. They are a valuable tool for acquiring information. In addition, our videos are used in educational settings to explain concepts, illustrate experiments or provide interactive lessons.
